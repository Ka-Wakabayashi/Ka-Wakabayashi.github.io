About

I am a theoretical physicist working on quantum materials, two-dimensional systems, and topological phenomena. My research focuses on electronic, optical, and transport properties of low-dimensional materials, including graphene, transition-metal dichalcogenides, moiré systems, and topological photonic structures.

Research Interests

  • Quantum transport in two-dimensional materials
  • Moiré exciton photoluminescence and disorder metrology
  • Topological photonics and lattice models
  • Quantum-information-inspired materials physics
